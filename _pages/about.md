I am a first-year PhD candidate at [HKUST NLP Group](https://github.com/HKUNLP), [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://hkust.edu.hk/), advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://jxhe.github.io/). I received my B.Eng. from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024.

My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning. My research interests include:
- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
